Manim is a powerful Python library that is used to create animations programmatically. It gained popularity after its debut on the 3B1B Youtube channel. This library was first written by Grant Sanderson, the founder of 3B1B Youtube channel. Since going public, this library has been regularly updated by a dedicated community of people who are passionate about science and maths education.
This library is incredibly versatile. It can be used to create a visualization for just about anything. From fancy equations to flow fields and linear transformations. The video files manim produces are extremely high quality. It supports up to 4K render resolution. On top of this, the video files are incredibly lightweight for what they offer.

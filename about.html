Why does this website exist?

My name is Itgel Ganbold and I am the author of this website. I am an MSc Computer Science student at UCD, Ireland. I have a BSc in Theoretical Physics from the same university. As part of my Web Development module in university, I was tasked to create a personal website. I chose the topic of Manim and Physics as I find animating physics concepts really fun and rewarding. Below is my LinkedIn and personal CV.

During my undergraduate studies at UCD, I did two internships with the Department of Physics. My job was to create physics explainer videos for the 1st and 2nd-year undergraduate students in our department. This is where I decided to learn about Manim. I felt that throughout my undergraduate years there were concepts and ideas I wish were explained to me in a visual way. This is also where I discovered that coding can be fun and rewarding. This is a major reason why I decided to pursue further education in Computer Science and not Physics. Although I still have a passion for learning about Physics. Coding the animations is only a small part of the whole video-making process. The entire process is as follows:

  1. Write the script for the video.
  2. Create the necessary animations using Python and Manim.
  3. Pick a suitable background music.
  4. Create necessary diagrams and still images.
  5. Record the narration using Audacity software .
  6. Edit the video in HitFilm Express software .
